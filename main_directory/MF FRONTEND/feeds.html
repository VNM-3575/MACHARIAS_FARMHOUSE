Nutrition Fundamentals

Pig nutrition must be tailored to the production stage (weaner, grower, finisher) to ensure optimal health and growth. Our feeds are formulated by nutrition experts to meet every pig's needs.

⚡ Energy

Derived from grains (maize, barley, wheat) and fats. Essential for growth, reproduction, and daily activity.

💪 Protein (Amino Acids)

Lysine is the most critical amino acid for muscle development. Requirements decrease as the pig ages.

🔬 Digestive Efficiency

For piglets, highly digestible vegetable proteins and rice flour are preferred as their guts are still immature.

🧂 Vitamins & Minerals

Essential for bone development, immunity, and overall health. Our feeds include balanced mineral profiles.

Our Feed Programs by Production Stage

🐤 Starter Feed (Piglets 0–8 weeks)

Highly digestible, nutrient-dense formulation with emphasis on protein and energy for rapid growth. Include probiotics for gut health support.

📈 Grower Feed (8–16 weeks)

Balanced nutrition supporting steady growth. Reduced protein levels as animals mature, optimized for feed conversion efficiency.

🎯 Finisher Feed (16 weeks – Market Ready)

Formulated to support rapid weight gain with adequate energy and moderate protein. Result: High-quality meat at optimal market weight.

👩‍🌾 Breeding Herd Nutrition

Special formulations for sows and boars supporting reproduction, milk production, and herd longevity.
