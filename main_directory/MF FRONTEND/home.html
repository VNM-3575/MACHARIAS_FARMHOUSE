About Us

Macharia's farmhouse is a modern family-owned farm and community dedicated to raising healthy pigs for breeding and market. We focus on animal welfare,technology intensive management, and sustainable practices to provide high-quality stock and services to farmers and customers in Kenya. clean facilities, and proven breeding lines to help farmers and customers get consistent, high-quality stock.

Our staff are experienced in feeding regimes, disease prevention, and biosecurity — ensuring animals are productive and well-cared for.
